HISTORY OF TNSCERT

The State Institute of Education (SIE) was established in 1965 to provide for systematic study of problems relating to School Education under the administration of Directorate of School Education

  • 1970

    State Institute of Education (SIE) was upgraded as State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT)

  • 1990

    The Government of Tamil Nadu created the Directorate of Teacher Education, Research and Training (DTERT) , by orders wide G.O.748, Education (F2) Department, dated 04.06.1990. The SCERT was renamed as DTERT.

  • 1997

    The entire academic faculty in the DIETs and DTERT have been separated from the Directorate of School Education and inducted into DTERT service vide G.O.No.106, School Education Department, dated 11.03.97

  • 1999

    All Teacher Training Institutions including Private Aided as well as Unaided were brought under its control. DTERT became a separate Unit

  • 1999

    All Teacher Training Institutions including Private Aided as well as Unaided were brought under its control. DTERT became a separate Unit

  • 2012

    DTERT was upgraded into “State Council of Educational Research and Training” vide G.O 83. School Education Department, dated 26.03.2012.
